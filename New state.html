Renault Austral: comfortable, technological and there is the new engine E-TECH Full Hybrid

Compared to the previous model, Renault Austral launches a new range of petrol engines. The 1.2 three-cylinder makes its debut, declined both in a 48-volt mild hybrid “advanced” version that delivers 130 hp. And then there's the big news. The 1.2 three-cylinder E-TECHfull hybrid at 400 volts that delivers 200 hp. The latter has a small 2 kWh battery and a 50 kW electric motor.
